Logo styling

The above are some technology/ai related company logos, which I quite like. The first logo, collibra uses a lines and dots, which are very common in AI related graphics because of brain/neural networks. You can choose to use dots and lines, but it is not a requirement from our side. Style wise, I prefer something that is more of a modern and minimal design. It does not have to be very simple(example collibra), but I prefer not to have 3D effects or unnecessary elements that makes it too messy.

We also would like to put the focus on our technologies, and not on our name iAmbition, which does not really have any connection to what we do. I would put AI/Big data as our core technology, they can be grouped under 'data science'.

While the name "iAmbition" should not be the focus of the logo, we are also a company does R&D on emerging technologies and one of our core values is to be BOLD, as such if possible, there should be some boldness in our branding. However you can consider the short name of our company, iAT/iATech if you want to include it in the design, but the logo should still reflect on data science/ai/big data.
